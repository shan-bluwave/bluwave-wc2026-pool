Rules & Scoring

How It Works

  1. 1 Before each knockout round begins, predict the winner of each match.
  2. 2 You earn points for each correct prediction. Points double every round.
  3. 3 Predictions must be submitted before the first match of each round kicks off.
  4. 4 The player with the most total points at the end of the tournament wins!

Points Per Round

Round Matches Points/Correct Pick Max Points
Round of 32 16 2 32
Round of 16 8 4 32
Quarter-Finals 4 8 32
Semi-Finals 2 16 32
Final 1 32 32
Maximum Total 160

Important Rules

  • • Round-by-round predictions: You only predict the upcoming round. No need to predict the entire bracket in advance.
  • • Deadline: Predictions lock when the first match of that round kicks off.
  • • Updates allowed: You can change your predictions any time before the deadline.
  • • Extra time & penalties: The team that advances counts as the winner (regardless of whether it went to penalties).
  • • Join anytime: Missed a round? No problem — you can still participate in later rounds.
  • • Tiebreaker: If two players have the same total points, the player who got more predictions correct in later rounds (Final > SF > QF > R16 > R32) ranks higher.

💡 Tip

Each round is worth the same total points (32). This means even if you miss early rounds, a perfect Final prediction (32 pts) is worth as much as getting all 16 matches right in the Round of 32. Stay in the game!
